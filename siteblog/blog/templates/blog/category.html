Issyk-Kul

The largest lake in Kyrgyzstan, without drainage, is one of the 30 largest lakes in the world by area and in seventh place in the list of the deepest lakes. It is located in the northeastern part of the republic, between the ridges of the Northern Tien Shan: Kyungoy-Ala-Too and Terskey Ala-Too at an altitude of 1608 m above sea level.
